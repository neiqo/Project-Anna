Temperature guide — bedroom context
Range Feel Indoor context vs. outside
Below 22°C Cool Air-con running cold, or early morning before sunrise Rare outside at night — Singapore rarely dips below 23°C even at 3am
22 – 25°C Comfortable Ideal sleep temperature. Air-con set to 23–25°C achieves this Outside at this temp = light rain just happened or 5–6am pre-dawn. Room is cooler than outdoors
25 – 28°C Warm, neutral Air-con off but fan on, or air-con set high. Feels neutral if humidity is low Outside at this range = overcast afternoon or post-rain. Room is roughly matching outdoors
28 – 31°C Warm, stuffy No airflow, fan not enough. Sticky if humidity is above 70% Outside at this temp = typical sunny afternoon. Room is at outdoor daytime level — no insulation benefit
Above 31°C Hot Unventilated room with windows shut mid-day. Air feels thick Hotter than a shaded outdoor spot — heat is trapped. Open windows or turn on air-con immediately
Humidity guide — bedroom context
Range Feel Indoor context vs. outside Level
Below 40% Dry Air-con has been running a long time. Throat and skin may feel dry. Unusual for SG Outside is almost never this dry in Singapore — room is drier than any outdoor condition here
40 – 60% Comfortable Ideal sleep range. Air-con achieves this. Feels fresh, no stickiness Outside only hits this during dry northeast monsoon (Dec–Feb) on clear days. Room is much drier than typical outdoors
60 – 70% Slightly humid Fan mode or light air-con. Noticeable but not uncomfortable if temperature is below 27°C Matches outdoor conditions on a cloudy morning or just after rain. Room humidity is equalising with outside
70 – 80% Humid Windows open during daytime. Sweat does not evaporate well. Feels muggy above 28°C Typical Singapore outdoor daytime humidity. Room has fully equalised with outside — no insulation benefit
Above 80% Very humid During or just after heavy rain with windows open. Everything feels damp. Mould risk if sustained Matches outdoor air during active rainfall. Room is absorbing outdoor humidity — close windows
